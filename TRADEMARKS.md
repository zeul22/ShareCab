# Trademark and Brand Policy

The ShareCab name, logo, app icon, screenshots, visual identity, slogans, domain
names, and product trade dress are not licensed under the repository source
license.

## Allowed Uses

You may use ShareCab marks only to:

- Refer truthfully to the upstream ShareCab project.
- Link to the official repository.
- State that your work is a fork or modification of ShareCab.
- Include screenshots in reviews, documentation, bug reports, or commentary
  about the project.

## Not Allowed Without Written Permission

You may not:

- Publish an app, service, package, website, or business using the ShareCab name
  or confusingly similar branding.
- Use ShareCab brand assets to imply endorsement, partnership, certification, or
  official status.
- Use ShareCab screenshots, app icons, or product marks in commercial marketing.
- Register domains, app-store listings, social handles, or company names that
  imply affiliation with ShareCab.

## Forks

Forks must use a clearly different name and branding if distributed outside a
private development environment.
